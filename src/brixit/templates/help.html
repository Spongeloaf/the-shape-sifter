Part dimensions may be entered as digits separated by 'x', '*', or 'by'. The order of the values does not matter either, "2x4" is equivalent to "4x2". Some parts have three dimensions, simply write the m out like "3x2x5". Whitespace is optional for dimensions; "2 x 4" and "2x4" are both acceptable. For example, "2 x 4" would be equivalent to "4by2" or "2* 4", and "2 x 4 x 8" would be equivalent to "8by2 x 4" or "4 * 8 by2".
